About Us

Bedfordshire is a county in the East of England.The historic market town of Bedford has many attractive old buildings and a wealth of wonderful places to explore.

Take a cruise down the River Great Ouse, visit one of Bedford's museums or historic sites, see a show, join a guided tour or simply relax in the town's beautiful parks and gardens.

Travel further afield and delight in the grandeur of Woburn Abbey and glide over Dunstable Downs, or watch a vintage aircraft show at Shuttleworth. Shop for antiques in Ampthill and find out about Catherine of Aragon's involvement with the village. Or relax in the beauty of Ashridge Forest.

FIND US

Bedfordshire is easy to reach by car, and public transport.

We are located between the A1 and M1 and are well served by the A421 dual carriageway, which links the two major roads as well as the A6 and A428.

Bedfordshire is served by Thameslink, East Midlands and the Marston Vale Line.
